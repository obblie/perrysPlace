Property Overview

My fully furnished studio has high ceilings, all-day bright sunlight, exposed brick walls, hardwood floors, and a huge newly renovated kitchen. It's on the 4th floor (top-floor walkup) of a beautiful pre-war building in a very green and spacious part of Crown Heights.

Utilities are included in rent, and the space is designed to feel beautiful, convenient, and fun for both short and flexible stays.
